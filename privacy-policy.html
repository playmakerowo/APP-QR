Privacy Policy

QR Reader App  ·  Effective date: May 8, 2026

Overview

QR Reader App is a personal utility app for scanning, generating, and managing QR codes. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties.

Camera Access

The app requests access to your device camera solely to scan QR codes and barcodes in real time. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never stored, recorded, or transmitted externally.

Gallery Access

The app may request access to your photo gallery to allow scanning QR codes from existing images. Selected images are processed locally and immediately discarded after scanning. No images are saved or uploaded.

Local Storage

Scanned QR code data (text and timestamp) is saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not synced to any server or cloud service
  • Can be deleted at any time from within the app
  • Is permanently removed when the app is uninstalled

No Data Collection

QR Reader App does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Crash reports sent to external services

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with any external service.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13.
